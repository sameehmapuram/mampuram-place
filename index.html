About Mampuram

A Sacred Pilgrimage Site

Mampuram Maqam is located in Tirurangadi Taluk of Malappuram district, Kerala, India. The exact address is:

Mampuram Maqam,
Abdul Rahman Nagar Panchayath,
Tirurangadi, Malappuram District,
Kerala, India - 676306

Geographical Location:
Mampuram is situated near the Kadalundi River, approximately 6 km south of Tirurangadi town. The coordinates are 11.0429° N latitude and 75.8707° E longitude.

Accessibility:
The site is well-connected by road and is about 30 km from Kozhikode (Calicut) city. The nearest railway station is Tirurangadi (6 km away), and the nearest airport is Calicut International Airport (approximately 35 km away).

Key Features

Mampuram Maqam

The sacred shrine of Sayyid Alavi Thangal, a revered Islamic scholar and spiritual leader, attracting thousands of devotees annually.

mampuram River

The serene Kadalundi River flows near the Maqam, adding to the spiritual ambiance and natural beauty of the location.

Historical Significance

Site of important historical events during the British colonial period and the Malabar Rebellion.

Educational Hub

Home to several educational institutions including the Mampuram Arabic College, continuing Thangal's legacy of learning.

Local Cuisine

Experience authentic Malabari Muslim cuisine at local eateries near the Maqam.

Annual Festivals

Witness grand celebrations during the annual Urs festival and other important Islamic occasions.
